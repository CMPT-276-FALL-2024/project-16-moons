About The Project

Whether you’re a home cook or a foodie, Dish-It is a website that gives you the freedom to get creative in the kitchen with a click. Dish-it’s goal is to help people save time, eat healthier, and explore different types of cuisines with a simple search. Along with that it also provides users a way to analyze what exactly is going in their dish in terms of nutrients.
